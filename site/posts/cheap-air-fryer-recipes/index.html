Delicious and Cheap Air Fryer Recipes to Try Today

Published 2025-09-01

Category: Cooking  ·  Tags: • air fryer • recipes • budget-friendly

Easy Air Fryer Veggies

Toss your favorite seasonal vegetables with olive oil and spices. Air fry at 400°F for 10-15 minutes, shaking halfway through.

This method enhances flavor while keeping costs low.

Budget Chicken Wings

Marinate chicken wings in soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. Air fry at 380°F for 25-30 minutes, flipping once.

This recipe is both affordable and perfect for game day.

Crispy Potato Wedges

Cut potatoes into wedges, coat with a mix of olive oil and seasoning, then air fry at 400°F for 20 minutes.

A cost-effective side dish that pairs well with any meal.

FAQ

Are air fryers energy-efficient?

Yes, air fryers generally use less energy than conventional ovens.

Can I make desserts in an air fryer?

Absolutely! You can prepare many desserts like brownies or fruit crisps.
