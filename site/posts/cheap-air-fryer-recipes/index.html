5 Cheap Air Fryer Recipes for Budget-Friendly Meals

Published 2025-09-01

Category: Cooking  ·  Tags: • air fryer • budget recipes • easy meals

1. Crispy Air Fryer Potatoes

Dice potatoes, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Air fry at 400°F for 15-20 minutes, shaking halfway through.

Enjoy crispy potatoes as a side or snack!

2. Air Fryer Vegetable Medley

Combine seasonal vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, and carrots. Season and air fry at 375°F for 10-15 minutes.

A healthy, colorful dish perfect for any meal.

3. Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Season chicken thighs with spices. Air fry at 380°F for 25-30 minutes until cooked through.

Juicy and flavorful, great for meal prep.

4. Air Fryer Banana Chips

Slice bananas thinly, toss with a sprinkle of cinnamon. Air fry at 350°F for 10-15 minutes.

A delicious, guilt-free snack.

5. Air Fryer Stuffed Peppers

Fill halved bell peppers with rice, beans, and spices. Air fry at 360°F for 15-20 minutes.

Healthy, filling, and easy to customize.
