Air Fryer vs Convection Oven: Which is Better?

Published 2025-09-03

Category Kitchen Appliances   Tags air fryer convection oven cooking

Overview of Air Fryers

Air fryers use rapid air technology to circulate hot air around food, creating a crispy exterior.

They typically cook food faster than traditional methods and use less oil.

Overview of Convection Ovens

Convection ovens also circulate hot air, but they are larger and can cook more food at once.

They are versatile, functioning as standard ovens with the added benefit of faster cooking.

Key Differences

Air fryers are generally more compact and energy-efficient for small batches.

Convection ovens can accommodate larger meals, making them suitable for families.

FAQ

Can I use an air fryer as a convection oven?

Yes, an air fryer can mimic convection oven functions but may be limited in capacity.

Are air fryers healthier than convection ovens?

Both appliances can prepare healthier meals, but air fryers typically use less oil.
