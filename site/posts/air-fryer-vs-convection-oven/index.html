Air Fryer vs Convection Oven: Which is Better?

Published 2025-09-01

Category: Appliances  ·  Tags: • air fryer • convection oven • cooking

What is an Air Fryer?

An air fryer uses rapid air circulation to cook food, creating a crispy texture similar to frying.

It typically requires less oil, making it a healthier option for frying.

What is a Convection Oven?

A convection oven uses a fan and an exhaust system to circulate hot air around food for even cooking.

It can bake, roast, and broil, making it a versatile kitchen appliance.

Key Differences

Air fryers are generally smaller and heat up quickly, ideal for quick meals.

Convection ovens offer larger cooking capacity and can handle multiple dishes at once.

FAQ

Can I use an air fryer as a convection oven?

Yes, many air fryers use convection technology, but they may not replace a full-sized convection oven.

Which is healthier, air fryer or convection oven?

Air fryers usually require less oil, making them a healthier choice for frying.
