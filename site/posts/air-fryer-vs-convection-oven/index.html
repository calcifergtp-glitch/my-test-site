Air Fryer vs Convection Oven: Which is Better?

Published 2025-08-30

Cooking Technology

Air fryers use rapid air technology to cook food quickly with minimal oil.

Convection ovens circulate hot air using a fan for even cooking and browning.

Cooking Time and Efficiency

Air fryers typically cook food faster than convection ovens due to their smaller size and concentrated heat.

Convection ovens may take longer, but they can handle larger batches of food at once.

Versatility

Air fryers excel at making crispy foods and reheating leftovers.

Convection ovens can bake, roast, and broil, offering more cooking options overall.

Space and Capacity

Air fryers are compact, making them ideal for small kitchens.

Convection ovens are larger and can accommodate multiple dishes but take up more counter space.

FAQ

Can I bake in an air fryer?

Yes, many air fryers come with baking accessories and can bake smaller items.

Which is healthier, air fryer or convection oven?

Air fryers typically use less oil, making them a healthier option for frying.
